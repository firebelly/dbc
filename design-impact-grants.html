At its heart, Designing a Better Chicago honors the work of all those who use design to advance civic good.

{% endcontentfor %}

The Design Impact Grant Program offers annual project-specific grants to individuals and organizations using design or design principles to directly address pressing issues in Chicago communities. Every year, 2-4 grants, up to $25K each, will be awarded based on the quality of applications and available funds.
